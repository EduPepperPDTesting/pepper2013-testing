To receive professional development credit for your Pepper course, from the Teacher’s College of San Joaquin please follow these easy steps.

  1. Download the Course Completion Form and fill out the required information
  2. Print a copy of your Course Certificate from your Pepper dashboard
  3. Make a check for $75 for one unit out to San Joaquin County Office of Education

And finally … mail the form, the certificate and the check to:

San Joaquin County Office of Education
Teacher Development Department/TCSJ
Attn: Admissions/Esmeralda Capello
P.O. Box 213030
Stockton, CA 95213-9030

Important note: Please check with your Human Resources Department to confirm that these credits are applicable for certification and salary growth opportunities.

For more specific questions, please contact Esmeralda Capello at ecapello@sjcoe.net.
