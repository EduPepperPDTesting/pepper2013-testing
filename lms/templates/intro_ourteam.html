WHO WE ARE

WestEd, a national nonpartisan, nonprofit research, development, and service agency, works with education and other communities to promote excellence, achieve equity, and improve learning for children, youth, and adults.
Pepper is delivered on the powerful edX learning platform, the Cambridge-based education partnership founded by Harvard University and M.I.T., to support two important missions: improving online education, and advancing teaching and learning through research.
PCG Education is a division of Public Consulting Group, currently works with more than 4,400 school districts and has contracts with 14 state education agencies to provide services and software solutions that focus on student achievement.
Edu2000 develops interactive content for state departments of education, school districts, and educational publishers. Edu2000 is particularly strong in the delivery of online mathematics and science curriculum that includes interactive animations, simulations, and visual experiments that teach concepts to all learners.
